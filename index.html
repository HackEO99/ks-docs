Kingswood Scout History Since 1914

Join us as we celebrate over a century of adventure, camaraderie, and service. Since 1914, the Kingswood Scouts have been dedicated to fostering leadership, community spirit, and outdoor skills in Scouts of Sri Lanka. Discover our rich history, memorable milestones, and the enduring legacy of our scouts who have thrived through the decades. Explore the timeless values and exciting activities that continue to shape future generations.
